## Overview

This guide will help you enable IPv6. Remember that you might need to configure your application to listen on IPv6.

## Enabling IPv6

1. Expand your environment. If you hover over "Public IP", an icon for attaching or detaching IP-addresses will appear.

![Hover-icon](/img/jelastic-paas/guides/jel_enable-ipv6-1.png)

2. Here we can choose to attach IPv4-addresses or enable Public IPv6 by switching the toggle to "ON".

![Enable-ipv6](/img/jelastic-paas/guides/jel_enable-ipv6-2.png)

3. Verify that your environment have been assigned a new IPv6-address.

![Verify-ipv6](/img/jelastic-paas/guides/jel_enable-ipv6-3.png)

That's it! If something in this guide is unclear or if you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
